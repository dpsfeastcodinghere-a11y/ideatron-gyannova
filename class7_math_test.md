## Questions:

### Integers
1.  Evaluate: (-8) + (-5) - (-3).
2.  The product of two integers is -120. If one integer is 15, find the other integer.
3.  **MCQ:** Which of the following statements is true?
    a) -5 > -2
    b) 0 < -1
    c) -10 < -12
    d) -7 < 0

### Fractions and Decimals
4.  Simplify: (3/4) + (1/2).
5.  Convert 0.65 into a fraction in its simplest form.
6.  **MCQ:** What is the value of 2.5 x 0.4?
    a) 10.0
    b) 1.0
    c) 0.1
    d) 0.01

### Data Handling
7.  Find the mean of the following data: 10, 12, 15, 13, 10.
8.  What is the mode of the data set: 2, 3, 5, 2, 4, 2, 5?
9.  **MCQ:** The difference between the highest and the lowest observation in a data set is called:
    a) Mean
    b) Median
    c) Range
    d) Mode

### Simple Equations
10. Solve for x: 3x + 7 = 16.
11. If 5 is subtracted from three times a number, the result is 10. Find the number.
12. **MCQ:** Which of the following is a linear equation in one variable?
    a) x² + 2 = 0
    b) 2x + 3y = 5
    c) 4x - 1 = 7
    d) x/y = 2

### Lines and Angles
13. If two lines intersect, and one of the angles formed is 60°, what are the measures of the other three angles?
14. What are complementary angles? Give an example.
15. **MCQ:** The sum of angles on a straight line is:
    a) 90°
    b) 180°
    c) 270°
    d) 360°

### The Triangle and its Properties
16. The angles of a triangle are in the ratio 1:2:3. Find the measure of each angle.
17. In a right-angled triangle, if the two shorter sides are 3 cm and 4 cm, what is the length of the hypotenuse?
18. **MCQ:** The sum of the lengths of any two sides of a triangle is always:
    a) Equal to the third side
    b) Less than the third side
    c) Greater than the third side
    d) Half of the third side

### Comparing Quantities

### Rational Numbers
